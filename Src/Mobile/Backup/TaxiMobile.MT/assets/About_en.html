About Taxi Diamond

L'Association de Taxi Diamond de Montréal Ltée is the largest provider of taxi dispatch services in Québec, the second largest in Canada and is among one of the largest taxi companies in North America. Founded in 1922, Diamond Taxi provides its services through more than 1,000 vehicles under four different banners. Diamond taxi and its members provide private transportation services to over 3.5 million residents and tourists each year in Montreal.

Our services

  • Fast Service due to state of the art GPS technology
  • Courier & Delivery Services
  • Para transit services
  • Specialised services for seniors
  • Student transportation services
  • Payment by credit & debit accepted in all our vehicles

Meter rates and Airport fixed rates

There is a fixed rate determined by the Government for all customers of all taxi companies in Montreal. This fixed rate applies only to a trip between downtown and Pierre-Elliott-Trudeau (Dorval) airport in Montreal. The amount is $ 40 to the airport.

Please note that taxis cannot be called to pick up a customer at the airport. If you need a taxi from the Pierre-Elliot-Trudeau airport, there is a taxi stand at arrivals level.

The minimum fare from the airport is $ 17.

Downtown boundaries are as follows:
  • Westward: Atwater Avenue to the Lachine Canal and Bridge Street to the river
  • Eastward: Papineau Avenue
  • Southward: Saint Lawrence River
  • Northward: Pine Avenue, Cherrier Street and Sherbrooke Street
